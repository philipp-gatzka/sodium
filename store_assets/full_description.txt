Sodium is a simple, fast, and private recipe manager designed for home cooks who want to keep their favorite recipes organized without the clutter.

YOUR RECIPES, YOUR DEVICE

Unlike other recipe apps, Sodium stores everything locally on your device. No accounts, no cloud sync, no subscriptions. Your recipes are yours alone.

KEY FEATURES

Recipe Management
- Create recipes with ingredients, instructions, and cooking times
- Add prep time, cook time, and serving sizes
- Set difficulty levels (Easy, Medium, Hard)
- Mark favorites for quick access
- Organize with custom categories

Easy Organization
- Search recipes by title instantly
- Filter by categories or favorites
- Sort by name, date, cooking time, or difficulty
- Quick access to recently added recipes

Beautiful Design
- Clean, modern Material Design interface
- Light and dark themes
- Adapts to your system theme preference
- Fast and responsive on any device

Data Control
- Export all recipes to JSON for backup
- Share exports via email, cloud storage, or messaging
- No account required - ever
- Uninstall anytime with no data left behind

PRIVACY FIRST

We believe your recipes are personal. That's why Sodium:
- Collects zero personal data
- Has no analytics or tracking
- Requires no internet connection
- Never shares anything with anyone

Read our full privacy policy in the app settings.

PERFECT FOR

- Home cooks building a personal recipe collection
- Anyone tired of recipe apps with ads and subscriptions
- Privacy-conscious users who want offline-first apps
- Cooks who want simplicity over complexity

GET STARTED

1. Tap + to add your first recipe
2. Enter title, ingredients, and instructions
3. Add optional details like cooking time and servings
4. Save and start cooking!
